Welcome to "Hunt the Wumpus"!

 The Wumpus lives in a cave of rooms.
 Each room has 3 tunnels leading to other rooms.


Hazards:

 Bottomless pits - two rooms have bottomless pits in them.
 If you go there, you fall into the pit(and lose!)
 
 Super bats - two other rooms have super bats.
 If you go there, a bat grabs you and takes you to some other room at random.
 (Which may be troublesome)
 

Wumpus:

 The Wumpus is not bothered by hazards. Usually he is asleep
 Two things wake him up: you shooting an arrow or you entering his room
 
 If the wumpus wakes up he moves (p = .75) one room or stays still (p = .25)
 If he enters youre room he eats you and you lose!


Controls:

 Each turn you may move or shoot a crooked arrow
 Moving: You can move from one room to annother by using drag and drop.
 Arrows: You have 5 arrows. You lose when you run out. Each arrow
 
 can go from 1 to 3 rooms. You aim by marking the rooms to shoot thru with the 
 
 right mouse button hold down.
 
 If the arrow hits the wumpus, you win. 

 Marking Hazards: You can mark each room with the hazards you expect using 
 
 right click on it.
 
 When you are one room away from the wumpus or hazard, the computer says:
 Wumpus: "I smell the wumpus"
 Bat: "I hear a bat"
 Pit: "I feel a breeze"
